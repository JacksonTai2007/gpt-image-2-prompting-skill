# GPT Image 2 — Scene Cookbook

Optimized, ready-to-run recipes for specific high-value scenes the community has reverse-engineered. Each entry names **the one trick** that makes the scene work, then gives a copy-paste prompt. These are tighter and more opinionated than the broad templates in `templates.md` — use a recipe when the request matches a *specific* scene; use a template when it's a generic artifact type.

**People slot convention:** where a recipe depicts a person, the slot reads `[appearance — match to the user's language/cultural context unless specified; always editable]`. Never hard-code one ethnicity (see SKILL.md → *Depicting people*). Where the scene implies a culture (Hanfu, onsen, etc.), the scene context governs.

## Photoreal people

### 1. Film-stock editorial portrait
*Aspirational, "shot on film" social portraits.*
**Trick:** lead with a named film stock + a *specific mixed-lighting situation*, then decompose features, and always close with `no plastic skin, no watermark, no text`. The film stock and lighting do 80% of the realism.
```
[35mm film photography / analog film], [specific lighting situation, e.g. "harsh convenience-store fluorescent mixed with neon glow from outside" / "soft diffused window light, slight overexposure, pastel tones"], authentic film grain, [high / low] contrast, slight color cast, cinematic editorial style, [intimate medium shot / full-body], [appearance — match to user's context; editable] in their early 20s, decomposed features (eye shape, brow, nose, jaw), realistic skin with visible pores and subtle texture, natural dewy makeup, [hair description with loose strands], wearing [outfit with materials], [pose] in [setting], realistic reflections and believable background blur, authentic [film stock, e.g. Kodak Portra 400] color grading, no plastic skin, no watermark, no text --ar 9:16
```

### 2. Authentic amateur snapshot
*The anti-influencer "real photo" look that reads as un-staged.*
**Trick:** invoke a *humble capture device* ("RAW unprocessed iPhone photo", "old CCD camera, harsh flash") and explicitly demand ordinary, un-polished subjects. Perfection is the tell; ask for imperfection.
```
Completely RAW, unprocessed, unedited [iPhone photo / old CCD camera photo with harsh flash], grainy, [dim messy indoor / momentary motion blur], candid snapshot feeling, [subject — appearance match to context; everyday clothes, no influencer styling], natural ordinary posture, faces like real pedestrians not models, realistic imperfect lighting, [setting with mundane real-world details: stickers, posters, reflections], looks like an authentic life slice, no plastic skin, no watermark, no text
```

### 3. Lifestyle / mirror selfie
*Intimate, casual, "caught in the moment" personal photo.*
**Trick:** name the capture act ("taking a mirror selfie with a smartphone") and a soft golden-hour side light; keep styling deliberately casual.
```
A [appearance — match to context; editable] person, youthful natural face with realistic skin texture, [taking a mirror selfie with a smartphone / sitting on a cozy slightly messy bed], casual [loungewear], soft natural golden-hour light from a side window, warm moody cinematic atmosphere, 35mm lens, sharp focus on subject, beautifully blurred background bokeh, photorealistic, high resolution. Aspect ratio 3:4.
```

### 4. Consistency grid (idol 3×3)
*Multiple frames of the same person in one image — a photoshoot contact sheet.*
**Trick:** put "same person in all frames, consistent facial features and styling" *and* a unifying filter (e.g. "soft black-mist filter, lowered contrast, blooming highlights") so the nine frames read as one cohesive shoot.
```
9:16 vertical, [idol / editorial] portrait photoshoot, 3×3 grid (nine frames), SAME person in all images, consistent facial features and styling throughout, [appearance — match to context; editable], [varied poses and angles per frame], soft black-mist filter effect, lowered contrast, blooming highlights, subtle glow around light sources, photoreal skin texture, no plastic skin, no watermark, no text
```

### 5. Documentary street slice
*A believable candid group scene, "captured by a photographer."*
**Trick:** specify a precise time + place, give each person a distinct mundane action, and pile on environmental props; then forbid polish.
```
Ultra-realistic [time + place, e.g. "convenience-store entrance at 10 PM on a summer night"] group photo. [3–4] [people — appearance match to context] each doing a distinct ordinary action (holding drinks, on a phone, sitting on a plastic chair). [Lighting mix: bright interior light through glass + warm street lights + distant headlights]. Everyday clothes, no influencer styling, faces and postures like real pedestrians. Include real environmental details: freezer stickers, posters, trash cans, entrance mats, glass reflections. Looks like an authentic life slice, not concept art. No plastic skin, no watermark, no text.
```

## Posters & illustration

### 6. City-silhouette travel poster
*A premium city poster where a flowing element morphs into a panorama of landmarks.*
**Trick:** one dynamic "carrier" shape (a river, a wake, a ribbon, smoke) sweeps across the frame and *transforms into* a hand-painted panorama containing the city's named landmarks; lots of negative space; exact typography pinned.
```
A striking [season + year] city poster for [city], elegant celebratory mood, bold contemporary design. Clean [off-white textured] background with large negative space. A [carrier element, e.g. "single sculler's oar wake"] sweeps across in a dynamic calligraphic curve and gradually transforms into [the river / a ribbon] and then a dreamlike hand-painted panorama of [city]. Inside this flowing shape: [4–8 named iconic landmarks]. Soft [morning fog], [golden] light, subtle festive accents in [color + color], layered depth, sophisticated, visually powerful but not overcrowded. Elegant typography reads "[HEADLINE]" with a slogan "[SLOGAN]", text clear and beautifully composed, premium graphic design, 9:16.
```

### 7. Silhouette-world poster
*A character's profile silhouette filled with an entire world.*
**Trick:** "inside the silhouette grows a complete world and iconic scenes" + cinematic-poster-meets-watercolor styling + premium negative space. The silhouette is the frame; the world lives inside it.
```
A collectible epic poster featuring [character]'s side-profile silhouette. Inside the silhouette grows a complete world and iconic scenes from [their story]. Style: cinematic poster meets dreamlike watercolor illustration. Mood: quiet, grand, sacred, nostalgic. Paper grain, light haze, dry-brush strokes, premium negative space. [Optional small title "[TITLE]", exact and legible.] 9:16.
```

### 8. Vintage screen-print travel poster
*Retro 1950s–60s tourism poster.*
**Trick:** "screen-print texture" + "loose strokes and defined contours" + a high-contrast retro palette; frame the foreground with a local motif.
```
Modern pencil illustration of a vintage travel poster depicting [place], [panoramic scene], [period vehicle/detail], [local foreground motif framing the edges], warm sunlight, bold vibrant retro 1950s travel-poster colors, cinematic composition, high detail, screen-print texture, hand-drawn style with loose strokes and defined contours, high-contrast decorative palette. [Optional "[PLACE NAME]" in classic poster type, exact.]
```

### 9. Surface-integrated typography
*Giant text painted onto a real surface in dramatic perspective.*
**Trick:** the text conforms to a surface and foreshortens to the vanishing point ("surface-integrated, not floating"), partially occluded by foreground for depth.
```
Scene: [dramatic cinematic angle of a surface, e.g. "side view of a cross-sea bridge"]. Giant bold sans-serif text "[TEXT]" painted onto the surface, progressively foreshortened from near to far, letterforms conforming to the surface curvature, surface-integrated not floating, partially occluded by foreground elements for depth-layering. [Bright color + sharp outline], extreme perspective distortion aligned to the vanishing point. Cinematic lighting, slight motion blur, poster-grade dynamic integrated typography, modern advertising aesthetics. Text exact and legible.
```

### 10. Double-exposure ink portrait
*An Eastern-ink double-exposure poster with narrative inside the silhouette.*
**Trick:** stack three zones (enlarged head up top, full figure below, and a scene fused inside the silhouette) connected by mist / ink-diffusion / negative space into one top-to-bottom visual flow.
```
A double-exposure ink portrait poster of [person/character]. 9:16, premium cinematic-poster composition. Upper zone: enlarged head/face outline as the strongest recognition anchor. Lower zone: the same figure full-body, pose [stance/gaze]. Inside the silhouette: fuse [key scene / symbolic objects / narrative fragments / environmental texture] as a double-exposure. Connect the upper outline, inner collage and lower figure with mist, ink diffusion, flying-white edges and negative space into one top-to-bottom visual flow. Style: Eastern ink aesthetic + cinematic realism, restrained, rich but not cluttered, generous whitespace. [Optional small title/name, exact, like a poster seal.] Constraints: no hard collage, no over-filled background, no cheap wuxia FX, subject and figure must not fight for focus.
```

### 11. Watercolor editorial illustration
*Soft, dreamy, magazine-grade watercolor.*
**Trick:** "translucent washes + loose brushstrokes + cold-pressed paper texture + soft blur" in a named two-color range; keep composition minimal.
```
Watercolor illustration in a dreamy style of [subject], light impressionist aesthetic, loose brushstrokes and translucent washes in tones of [color1] and [color2], soft blur over cold-pressed paper texture, delicate lighting, clean minimalist composition, a sense of calm and ephemeral beauty, editorial quality.
```

### 12. Surreal scale-contrast illustration
*Dreamlike image driven by extreme scale difference.*
**Trick:** a tiny human (back to viewer) gazing up at a giant subject; low-angle upward perspective; ethereal atmosphere. The scale gap is the whole image.
```
A surreal digital illustration, low-angle upward perspective. A giant [subject, e.g. "colorful koi"] [action] through a dreamlike [environment, e.g. "nebula with star clouds and bubbles"]. In the center, a tiny human figure, back to viewer, calmly gazing up at the massive [subject] looming overhead. Strong scale contrast, ethereal dreamlike atmosphere. 9:16.
```

## Screens & game

### 13. Named-game screenshot
*Authentic-looking gameplay from a specific, named game.*
**Trick:** name the exact game (activates its art style via world knowledge) and, for extra realism, frame it as a *photo of a monitor*: "close-up handheld photo of a 4K monitor, slight blur."
```
[Named game, e.g. "GTA 6"] in-game footage, very detailed, very realistic, [scene + character action]. Close-up shot taken from a stationary 4K monitor (slight handheld blurriness, as if photographed). [Wide bright environment]. Authentic [game] HUD and art style. Realistic details.
```
*Variants:* drop the monitor framing for a clean screenshot; or describe a specific in-game moment ("[character] knocked back by [enemy] in [game]").

### 14. Full app screenshot
*A high-fidelity, publishable mobile app screen — not concept art.*
**Trick:** spell out the *complete UI logic* top-to-bottom (status bar at 9:41, nav, content modules, tab bar) and end with "must look like an authentic app screenshot, not concept art." Every text label hardcoded and readable.
```
High-fidelity [platform] [app type] screenshot, extremely realistic, complete mobile UI logic and commercial design sense. Top: status bar (time 9:41, 5G, battery). [Section-by-section content: e.g. "search box → promo banner → 10-icon grid → flash-sale module with countdown → double-column product waterfall, ≥6 cards each with image/title/price/sales/shop/rating" OR a music player: "blurred album-art background → large album cover → song/artist → progress bar with times → shuffle/prev/play/next/repeat → scrolling lyrics with current line highlighted"]. Bottom: fixed tab bar. All text clear and readable, unified icons, realistic whitespace, card shadows, rounded corners. Must look like an authentic app screenshot, not concept art. 9:16.
```

### 15. Live-stream screenshot
*A realistic livestream UI with an active audience.*
**Trick:** demand the full live-UI element set — viewer count, scrolling chat/danmaku, reaction icons, gift banners — over a believable creator + scene.
```
Realistic screenshot of a [TikTok / platform] live stream: a [creator — appearance match to context] streaming to an active audience. Show all standard live UI: viewer count, scrolling chat messages [in target language], floating reaction/like icons, gift banners, follow button. [Scene/lighting]. Believable phone-camera framing, all UI text readable. 9:16.
```

### 16. Social-feed mockup
*A simulated social app feed — including the popular historical-mashup gag.*
**Trick:** simulate a real feed (avatar, username, post text, attached image, likes, comments, status bar) with every string hardcoded; for the viral version, set all content in a historical period for comedic anachronism.
```
Simulate a [platform] mobile feed UI, [dark mode + palette]. Avatar: [portrait]. Username: "[name]". Post text: "[exact post]". Attached image: [description]. Likes: "[names] and N others". Comments: "[user]: [exact]" / "[user]: [exact]". Status bar: "[carrier] [signal]" and "[date]". All text exact and readable, authentic app layout, not concept art.
[Historical-mashup variant: set every element in [dynasty/era] — period avatar, period names, period-flavored post and comments, like-icon restyled to a period motif — for time-travel humor.]
```

### 17. UI design system (one shot)
*A whole component system in a single image.*
**Trick:** ask for the *system* — web + mobile + cards + controls + buttons — in one named style, so components share one visual language.
```
Generate a complete UI design system in [style, e.g. "glassy translucent / frosted glass with soft shadows"], including web pages, mobile screens, cards, controls, buttons and other components, all sharing one cohesive visual language, realistic spacing and shadows, sample labels clear and readable. Arrange as an organized component board.
```

## Character design

### 18. Official character reference sheet
*Studio-grade model sheet — best done image-to-image from one drawing.*
**Trick:** request the full deliverable set on a clean layout: three-view (front/side/back) + expression variations + clothing/equipment breakdown + color palette + worldview note. Provide a reference image for identity.
```
Based on [this character / the description], create a character reference sheet like official design materials:
- three-view drawings: front, side, back
- a row of facial-expression variations
- broken-out details of clothing and equipment
- a color palette swatch row
- a brief worldview/setting note
Organized layout, white background, [illustration / Persona-5-setting-material] style, high resolution, professional concept art. Keep the same character identity throughout.
```

### 19. Expression grid
*N panels of one character cycling through emotions.*
**Trick:** fix the grid and *list the exact expressions*; put "face shape, hairstyle, clothing must stay highly consistent across all panels" before the list.
```
Create a [16]-panel expression grid of [character — appearance/identity]. Face shape, hairstyle and clothing must stay highly consistent across all panels. Equal panels, hairline dividers, numbered. The [16] expressions: [happy, sad, angry, surprised, shy, speechless, evil grin, contemplative, curious, proud, wronged, disdainful, confused, scared, crying, heart]. Clean background, illustration style, do not change the character's identity.
```

### 20. Visual-novel intro page
*A galge-style character introduction "web page".*
**Trick:** lay it out like a real character-intro webpage: expression variations + a CG + a chibi + a stats block, all text hardcoded.
```
Create a high-quality visual-novel (galge) character introduction page that looks like a real website page. Include: facial-expression variations, one CG illustration, a chibi version, and this info block (exact text):
Name: [name]
Theme color: [color]
Height: [h] cm   Weight: [w] kg
Catchphrase: "[quote]"
Organized layout, consistent character identity, all text readable.
```

## Infographics & knowledge

### 21. Museum-catalog disassembly
*A national-museum exhibition-board infographic with a fixed, reliable layout.*
**Trick:** pin the exact fixed layout (title top / disassembly left / material upper-right / pattern+color middle-right / flowchart+summary bottom) on a paper-texture background, and explicitly say it is NOT a poster/portrait/e-commerce/anime.
```
Generate a "museum-catalog-style disassembly infographic" for [subject]. Combine a realistic main visual, structural disassembly with annotated lead lines, material/craft descriptions, pattern meanings, color meanings, and a core-feature summary. Style: national-museum exhibition board / historical catalog — NOT a poster, portrait, e-commerce page, or anime. Background: paper texture (off-white / silk / light tea). Fixed layout — Top: title + subtitle + intro. Left: structural disassembly with lead lines and close-up details. Upper-right: material / craft / texture samples. Middle-right: pattern + color palette + cultural explanations. Bottom: composition flowchart + core-feature summary. All text clear, neat, readable. Emphasize real structure, material differences, cultural explanation, catalog atmosphere.
```

### 22. Exploded technical breakdown
*An "exploded view" of a product/device with callouts.*
**Trick:** "exploded view, components separated along an axis, connected by thin lead lines to short numbered labels"; keep labels terse; pick a clean background.
```
Exploded-view technical breakdown of [product/device]. Components separated and floating along a [vertical/diagonal] axis in correct assembly order, thin lead lines connecting each part to a short numbered label. [Realistic product render / blueprint] style, [clean background]. [N] callouts, labels terse and readable. Optional title "[TITLE]" exact. Emphasize real structure and part relationships, no clutter.
```

### 23. Process-journey infographic
*Numbered stages of a process with data, as a display board.*
**Trick:** number the stages (01–0N), give each an icon + short title + concrete data points, add path arrows; end with "high-quality display board, not a classroom slide."
```
Infographic poster: "[How X happens / journey of Y]". High-end information design, educational + commercial appeal, clear layout with path arrows, data boxes, icons, simple illustrations, modular cards. Palette: [3–4 colors]. Stages: [01 Title — data; 02 Title — data; … 0N Title — data], plus [keyword row]. Must look like a high-quality display board, not a classroom slide. All labels short and readable.
```

### 24. Relationship map
*A character/entity relationship diagram as a design-forward poster.*
**Trick:** "design-forward relationship map poster" with nodes (portraits/labels) and labeled connecting lines showing relationship types; readable labels.
```
Generate a key relationship diagram for "[work/topic]" — a visually compelling, design-forward relationship-map poster showing all major [characters/entities], their relationships, and key connections. Nodes with [portraits/labels], labeled connecting lines indicating relationship type, clear hierarchy, readable labels, cohesive palette.
```

## Commercial & heritage

### 25. Text-hierarchy product poster
*A launch poster carrying a lot of small, precise, readable copy (tea, skincare, sales).*
**Trick:** explicitly enumerate the full text hierarchy (brand → product → series → tagline → prices → promo → fine print → QR) and demand "clear promotional hierarchy while staying sophisticated, not cheap e-commerce." This model can render dense small copy — lean into it.
```
Design a [3:4] [product] launch poster. Brand: "[brand]". Style: [aesthetic, e.g. "New Chinese, light-luxury, restrained" / "clean scientific skincare"]. Colors: [palette]. Main subject: [hero product with material/ingredient cues]. Accurately display this text hierarchy (all exact and readable): brand name, product name, series name, launch tagline, key ingredients/features, price (sizes), in-store promo, fine print "[disclaimer]", QR-code area. Requirements: clear promotional hierarchy while staying sophisticated, not cheap e-commerce. Focus on small text, numbers, prices, info modules, and font aesthetics.
```

### 26. Brand-mascot collab poster
*A consistent mascot/pet in a brand collaboration — image-to-image.*
**Trick:** "the same [mascot], absolutely consistent in appearance and coloring" + the partner brand's identity (colors, logo, scenes); put the mascot inside the brand world.
```
"[Mascot/pet] X [Brand]" collaboration poster. Feature the SAME [mascot] (absolutely consistent appearance and coloring) within [brand]'s identity (color scheme, classic logo, branded scenes). Have the [mascot] [wear brand uniform / interact with branded products]. Lively, fun, commercial-collab feel, suitable for promotion. [Optional exact tagline.] Keep the mascot's identity identical to the reference.
```

### 27. Ancient scroll / poetry
*A long-form traditional scroll visualizing a poem or text.*
**Trick:** use a wide handscroll ratio, lay the full text as elegant vertical calligraphy alongside an ink-painting scene, and treat the long text as a hardcoded design element (poems are short enough to render).
```
A traditional [handscroll, wide panoramic ratio] visualizing [poem/text title]. Ink-and-wash [or gongbi] painting of [the scene the text evokes] flowing left to right. The full text rendered as elegant [vertical] calligraphy in its correct characters, integrated as a design element beside the painting, exact and legible. [Period-accurate details], restrained refined palette, generous negative space, seals/colophon accents. Constraints: text correct and readable, no modern elements, cohesive ink aesthetic.
```

### 28. Hand-drawn watercolor map
*An illustrated, hand-painted city/area map.*
**Trick:** "hand-drawn watercolor illustrated map, bird's-eye, landmarks drawn as little painted icons with labels"; warm paper texture; labels readable.
```
A hand-drawn watercolor illustrated map of [city/area], gentle bird's-eye perspective. Landmarks drawn as charming little painted icons [list a few], streets and rivers as soft watercolor lines, labels in [language] hand-lettered and readable. Warm paper texture, soft palette, decorative compass/border accents. Looks like an artisan tourist map, not a screenshot.
```

### 29. Labeled item grid
*A grid of many unique, individually-labeled small items (pixel-art, stickers, icons).*
**Trick:** specify the grid size, that every cell is *unique and meaningfully labeled*, with consistent style and clean separators. Great stress-test of text + structure together.
```
A single image containing a [10×10] grid of [completely unique pixel-art items / stickers / flat icons], each in its own cell with a short readable label beneath it. Consistent [art style + palette], even spacing, hairline separators, clean background. Every item distinct and meaningful, every label exact and legible. [Aspect ratio].
```

## How to use a recipe
1. Find the closest scene; copy its prompt.
2. Fill every `[slot]`; for people, set the appearance slot per the user's context (and tell them it's editable).
3. Keep the recipe's trick and its constraints — they're the load-bearing parts.
4. Pin an aspect ratio. Add any extra negatives from `troubleshooting.md` if the first roll misbehaves.
5. For a *set* of these, wrap with the batch-orchestration instruction from SKILL.md → *Consistency & series*.
